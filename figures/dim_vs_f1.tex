\caption{\textbf{Effective dimensionality vs.\ Micro-F1.} Each marker is the
best run (across seeds) of one selection method on each dataset. The
\textcolor{gray!80}{dashed line} traces the Pareto frontier (lower dimension
\emph{and} higher F1 dominates). Standard ACE consistently lies on the right
half of every plot, using $\mathbf{1.6\times}$--$\mathbf{3\times}$ more
dimensions than the granular variants while \emph{never} achieving the highest
F1. The proposed \textbf{G-ACE} and \textbf{H-ACE} occupy the upper-left
region, confirming that block-level selection produces representations that are
both more compact and more accurate.}
